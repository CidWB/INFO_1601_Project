About Us

Welcome to Lettuce Eat! We're a team of passionate food enthusiasts who know the struggle of trying to find the right recipe for your next meal. Because of this, we aim to provide a simple and easy-to-use web application that allows users to easily search for recipes based on the ingredients they have in their fridge.
